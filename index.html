HOW TO PLAY

🎮 Controls

  • Move: Use A/D keys, Joystick, or Swipe Left/Right.
  • Jump: W key, Space, or Swipe Up.
  • Block: S key or Swipe Down to guard against attacks.
  • Light Attack: J key or A button (Fast strikes).
  • Heavy Attack: K key or B button (Slow, heavy damage).
  • Dash: L key or Dash button (Dodges attacks swiftly).
  • Skill: I key or Skill button (Special power attack).

🏆 Strategy to Win

  • Be Patient: Enemy attacks hit hard! Don't just repeatedly press attack. Let the opponent finish their combo, then strike back while they are paused.
  • Watch Your Stamina (Blue Bar): Dashing and using heavy skills drain stamina. If it empties, you can't dodge! It refills automatically over time.
  • Dodge and Counter: Wait for the enemy to step close, Dash backward to avoid their blade, then immediately Dash forward and use a Heavy Attack.
  • Master Combos: Tap the Light Attack button three times in a row for a fast 3-hit combo that keeps enemies stunned.
  • Upgrade at the Armory: Earn coins in matches and unlock better weapons. E.g., The Iron Spear has a long reach that keeps the warlords far away!
